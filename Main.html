History of Chemistry (very short!)

From the ancient practice of metallurgy and alchemy to Lavoisier’s 18th-century revolution defining elements and conservation of mass; from Dalton’s atomic theory and Mendeleev’s 1869 periodic system to the 20th-century quantum model explaining periodic trends; the discovery of noble gases, synthetic elements, and nuclear chemistry rounded out the modern table.
